Game Mechanics

  • Combo System: Pop normal bubbles to build multiplier (max 3x)
  • Bombs: Explode and pop nearby bubbles, triggering chains!
  • Power-ups: Slow Motion, 2X Points, or Shield
  • Waves: Game speeds up every 5 combo hits
  • Time: 60 seconds to get the highest score!
